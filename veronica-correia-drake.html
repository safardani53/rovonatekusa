VERONICA Correia went viral after tossing her bra at Drake during his concert at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn, New York, on July 21, 2023.

Since the concert, Correia has received new career opportunities and now fans want to know more about the concertgoers.

Who is Veronica Correia?

Veronica Correia is a 21-year-old Portuguese businesswoman from Rhode Island.

She currently owns a coffee shop named Café La La in Cumberland, Rhode Island.

Apart from being a business owner, Correia is also a mother of a 2-year-old daughter who was born on February 24, 2021.

Since going viral, Correia has built a following on social media.

Why is Veronica Correia trending?

Veronica Correia began trending after she threw her bra at Drake whilst he was walking on stage to perform.

Once Drake picked up the bra, he jokingly commented on the size of it saying: "36G? Locate this woman immediately!"

Correia then took to TikTok to reveal her identity in a video saying: "It's me! I threw it, and he picked that s**t up and said 36G, find this girl immediately."

Is Veronica Correia a Playboy model?

Since going viral, Playboy reached out to Correia via Instagram in hopes to recruit her to join their app as a creator.

The company wrote: "Hi Veronica! You are gorgeous! I'm part of the Playboy recruitment team and we love your look.

"We recently launched a new invitation-only Playboy app that gives creators the opportunity to earn by monetizing exclusive content similar to the ones you're already posting on IG and TikTok.

"We would love to invite you to apply. Let me know once you've applied and I'll accept you right away."

Correia kindly accepted the offer saying: "Hi! Thank you so much. I have applied and cannot wait to hear back!"

According to Correia, she is currently in contact with the company to talk about her near future career move.
