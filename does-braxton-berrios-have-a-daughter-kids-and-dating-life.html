Does Braxton Berrios Have A Daughter? Kids And Dating Life

The Miami Dolphins player, Braxton Berrios, is currently dating a TikTok star, Alix Earle. The player does not have any children as of now.

There has been some controversy around Braxton’s current relationship and his previous breakup.

As the NFL player has been making headlines with his dating life, people have been curious about his personal life, partner, and children (son/daughter).

Braxton Berrios, born on October 6, 1995, is an American football wide receiver for the Miami Dolphins of the NFL.

He attended Leesville Road and committed to play at the University of Miami as a four-star prospect.

Besides his athletic attributes, he excelled academically and graduated as the valedictorian with a double major in entrepreneurship and finance.

Berrios started his NFL career when the New England Patriots drafted him in the sixth round of the 2018 NFL draft.

After being waived by the Patriots, the Super Bowl LIII champion joined the New York Jets. He quickly became one of the key players and played for four seasons with the Jets.

Following his release from the Jets, he joined his home state team, signing a one-year contract with the Miami Dolphins.

Braxton Berrios Family, Children (Daughter/Son)?

The North Carolina native was born to his father, Rico Berrios, and his mother, Lee Berrios-Smothers.

The NFL player and his father seem to share their love for football, as Rico was a former college football player.

He has four siblings, the youngest one being 8 years old. His older brother, Austin Berrios, also played college football as a wide receiver.

At present, he has been working as a Regional Sales Manager for the DFW offices at CoAdvantage since March 2022.

Regarding his private life, Braxton is in a relationship but is not reported to have any kids. 

Who Is Braxton Berrios Dating

Lately, Braxton has been all over the media regarding his dating life. The professional football wide receiver is in a relationship with a TikTok star, Alix Earle.

Braxton Berrios previously dated Culpo, a model, reality TV star and host of Hot Mess. The pair dated for two years and parted ways in March 2023.

However, the breakup caused some controversy as there were some cheating allegations.

After being seen with Alix Earle on a couple of occasions, netizens started speculating on the sparking relationship between Braxton and Alix.

Alix also shared a picture of them on Instagram on Braxton’s 28th birthday, captioning, “HBD, NFL man.”

The TikTok star says they are seeing each other, but things are still casual for now.

However, Alix is seen supporting Braxton; she was recently seen at the Miami Dolphins game cheering for him.

The Breakup Controversy

Braxton Berrios and Alix Earle’s relationship has caused some controversies surrounding infidelity as his ex, Sophia Culpo, commented on their relationship.

While announcing their split on social media, Sophia said somebody close to the Jets informed her about the alleged infidelity.

On the other hand, Berrios denies the allegation and comments on the situation, saying, “Towards the end of it (relationship), we had conversations along the lines of we just weren’t working out.”

Similarly, Berrios’ current girlfriend states, “They had already broken up when we started seeing each other.”
