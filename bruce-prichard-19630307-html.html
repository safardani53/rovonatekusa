Bruce Prichard is an American podcaster and professional wrestling writer, commentator, manager and producer. As of August 2020, Prichard has been recruited by WWE, where he is the Senior Vice President and CEO of SmackDown and Raw. As an advertising manager, Prichard performed under the stage name Brother Love and was the original director of The Undertaker. During Prichard's competition as Brother Love, he hosted his own segment, The Brother Love Show, where he often assists heel wrestlers. After leaving WWE in 2008, Prichard served as senior vice president of programming and non-stop action wrestling talent relations. Since 2016, Prichard has co-hosted Something to Wrestle with Bruce Prichard's podcast with Conrad Thompson.
