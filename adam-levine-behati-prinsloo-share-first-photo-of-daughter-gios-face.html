Adam Levine, Behati Prinsloo Share First Photo of Daughter Gios Face

September 09, 2024
blog

That face! Behati Prinsloo officially shared a photo of daughter Gio Grace’s face for the first time as she penned a sweet tribute to hubby Adam Levine on Father’s Day.

“My first baby holding OUR second baby…..Our girls are so lucky to call you dad and I’m so lucky to have you as a husband a lover and a friend. We love you. Life is better with you,” the model captioned an Instagram pic of the Maroon 5 frontman holding their little one.

She added: “To my dad, thank you for being the best example to show me what i would want in a husband and father one day, the way you look at mom is the way Adam looks at me, thank you for being the best dad EVER.”

Prinsloo, 29, and Levine’s eldest daughter, Dusty Rose, 21 months, became a big sister when Gio arrived in February.
