Top five coins worth between $9,000 and $144,000 - do you have one in your spare change?

March 11, 2024
blog

PENNIES, quarters, and other coins have a lot more value than you think, and some could be worth as much as $144,000.

Some of the most valuable coins are mule error coins because of how rare they are.

A TikTok influencer, thecoinchannel, also known as Eric Miller, showed five different kinds of mule error coins that are worth thousands of dollars.

A mule is a coin struck from mismatched dies.

"Every coin I show you in this video is going to be worth a minimum of $9,000," Miller said.

Miller also captioned his video: "These are called MULE error. Where they are minted on wrong coins. Everything in this [video] has been certified and sold."

1. 2000 Lincoln Cent New Hampshire state quarter - $9,400

First, Miller showed a penny that was struck on a quarter, and its value was exponentially more.

"This is a 2000 penny that was struck on a New Hampshire quarter sold for $9,400," he said.

2. 1999 Lincoln Cent - $138,000

"Next is this 1999 Penny. On the back of the coin...if you look near 'UNITED,' you'll see letters from a different coin that's not supposed to be there.

"This is estimated at $50,000 and up," he said.

The edge of the coin should have some letters imprinted on them, so it can be easily identifiable.

Miller showed another 1999 Penny with the reverse of a dime, and it was sold for $138,000.

3. 2001 Denver penny with a Roosevelt Dime Reverse - $78,000

"Next we have a 2001 Denver penny, but the reverse is a Roosevelt dime. Sold this year for $78,000," he continued.

The coin is a copper-nickel coin, and it is one of the rarest coins as mismatched dies do not occur very often.

4. Washington Quarter Obverse with Sacagawea Reverse - $144,000

"This quarter got struck on the Sacagawea $1, sold for $144,000," Miller emphasized.

This mule coin was a state quarter and the brand new Sacagawea dollar struck together.

5. Sacagawea dollar minted on presidential dollar - $84,000

"Finally this Sacagawea dollar got minted on a presidential dollar," Miller said.

This error that paired a Sacagawea Dollar obverse with a Presidential Dollar reverse was struck in 2014 at the Denver Mint.

The coins are found on the Professional Coin Grading Service (PCGS).

PCGS is a coin grading and authentication company.

Other rare coins

There are other rare coins such as as the 1982 dime without a mint mark above the year, which routinely sells in the hundreds on eBay.

Another valuable error dime with a blank detail could be worth more than $7,000.

It was a dime minted onto a penny planchet.

In terms of key dates, one with the year 1916 and a specific letter mark could be worth up to $200,000.
