PARTY season is among us - which means millions of Brits are scrambling to get their Christmas ‘do outfits sorted last minute. 

And it turns out that supermarkets like Tesco, Sainsbury's and Asda are leading the way in Christmas party attire. 

One shopper recently nabbed a £30 dress from Asda for just £19 - and other buyers agree it’s the perfect outfit for your office party. 

Taking to Facebook, Marie Grantham shared her bargain buy. 

“May just be a fluke but this £30 dress in Asda scanned at just £19,” she said.

“I scanned my asda app barcode and then my £2 off George voucher code so still should have been £28.
