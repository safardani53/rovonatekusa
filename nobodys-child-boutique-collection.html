Nobody's Child Boutique's collection is a must have for summer - these are our fave pieces

July 29, 2024
blog

NOBODY'S CHILD is one of our go-to brands all year round, but particularly dress shopping, and summer dressing.

And ahead of summer, the eco-conscious brand has since launched the Nobody's Child Boutique collection, also found under the NC Boutique tab online, complete with all the summer essentials.

  • Shop Nobody's Child Boutique Collection - clickhere

The Nobody's Child Boutique Collection is available to shop online at Nobody's Child now.

The latest drop, which comes shortly after the third collection from Fearne Cotton, is a resort-inspired collection.

The 10-piece collection is made from pure organic cotton, which is soft to the touch, lightweight and breathable, all major ticks for a capsule summer wardrobe.

But what is most impressive is the attention to detail in every garment, from the precision on the needlework, which creates stunning embroidery to the vibrant colour palette.

We adore everything about the new drop, which has been described as a new line for the "refined bohemian", by Nobody's Child.

While the latest instalment is still a nod to the classic Nobody's Child designs we know and love, this capsule goes a little further, as it is bolder with colours, textures, and detailing.

Focusing on daisy chain embroidery, cut out detailing, floral designs, as well as ladder lace and much more, which sets this range a cut above the rest.

The collection includes mini dresses, as well as midi/ midaxi designs, and two-piece co-ords, so there is plenty to see you through any upcoming holidays, festivals, summer parties, or other occasions.

Like all Nobody's Child creations you can dress up, or down, pair the designs with flat sandals, or heeled wedges, opt for trainers or heels, to take you from day to night in an instant.

The Nobody's Child Boutique line ranges in price from £65 to £120, all available on Nobody's Child's website now.

We want everything in the range, but we have whittled down a selection of our absolute die hard essentials.

Shop our top picks from Nobody's Child Boutiques range

White Broderie Anglaise Jessie Midi Dress

No summer wardrobe is complete without broderie anglaise, or a white dress, combine the two, and Nobody's Child Boutique has you covered.

This design is lightweight, classy, elegant, and perfect for any occasion, whether it is work, a picnic in the park, a summer wedding or christening, or a shopping spree.

From the short puff sleeve, and delicate rounded collar, to the button front fastening and belt detailing, there are a lot of elements, which work together and make for a super stylish creation.

  • White Broderie Anglaise Jessie Midi Dress, £120 from Nobody's Child - buy here

White Embroidered Sweetheart Josie Top

We love the Sweetheart Josie Top, which is perfectly paired with the matching midi skirt to complete the co-ord.

Alternatively pair with denim jeans, shorts, or skirt, to get more wear out of this creation.

The sweetheart neckline, puff sleeve, tie front bodice style, and subtle lace hem, makes for a super flattering, and uber feminine design, to see you through spring and summer.

  • White Embroidered Sweetheart Josie Top, £65 - buy here

White Embroidered Summer Midi Skirt

Come the summer months we are desperate to get out of restrictive jeans in favour of more lightweight fabrics and floaty designs, and this midi skirt ticks those boxes.

It boasts the same floral embroidered detail as the Josie Top, and features tiered detailing for a flattering midi skirt.

It comes high waisted and falls to the ankles, so it can be perfectly paired with sandals, trainers, or wedges.

Similar to the top, you can pair together or mix and match with t-shirt, camis, vests, blouses, anything you feel comfortable in. You can even go wild and pull out the colours in the floral detail to make a bold statement.

  • White Embroidered Summer Midi Skirt, £85 - buy here

Yellow Broderie Anglaise Paris Mini Dress

We've mentioned before how broderie anglaise screams summer, and so do cut out details on dresses, as well as this buttercup yellow hue.

This design is available to shop in the mini version, as well as midaxi, but without the cut out and hoop detail in the centre, so you can find the perfect style to suit your preferences.

We love the mini dress as it has a lot of character, is fun, and playful, perfect for your holidays, city breaks, and weekends basking in the rays.

  • Yellow Broderie Anglaise Paris Mini Dress, £95 - buy here

Black Broderie Anglaise Nora Midaxi Dress

The Black Broderie Anglaise Nora Midaxi Dress will be a wardrobe staple every summer, it's a timeless classic and one we can't get enough of.

From the balloon sleeves, to the slight smock detail, v-neck and elasticated waist back, makes this a super simple, but flattering design for every shopper.

The black make sit timeless, and appropriate for all occasions - even if you're shopping for a wedding guest dress this summer.

  • Black Broderie Anglaise Nora Midaxi Dress, £120 - buy here

Brown Embroidered Starlight Midaxi Dress

The Brown Embroidered Starlight Dress comes in this warming rust hue, and contrast floral embroidered stitching, as well as soft dusk pink with yellow stitching for a true summer feel.

What we love is both colours add a little vibrancy to your closet.

The puff sleeve, v-neckline, combined with the button front fastening and cinched in waist detail before floating out into an A-line design, makes this flattering in all the right ways.

You can wear with wedges or heels, or sandals, to every occasion. We plan to live in it all summer long.

  • Brown Embroidered Starlight Midaxi Dress, £120 - buy here
