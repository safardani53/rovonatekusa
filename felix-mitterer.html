Felix Mitterer Age, Biography

Felix Mitterer was born on February 6, 1948. He is originally from Austria. As a result, he has Austrian nationality and is Austrian-White. He was the thirteenth child of Adelheid Marksteiner, a widowed farm worker, and an unknown, presumably Romanian refugee, and was adopted by an underprivileged farm worker couple who were friends of his mother at the time. He grew up and attended school in Kitzbühel and Kirchberg.

Felix then attended Innsbruck’s teacher training college. He finished his education after three years. Then he escaped and took the train to Rotterdam. From 1995 to 2010, he worked and lived in Castlelyons (Caisleán Liatháin), Ireland. In order to relocate to Austria, he purchased a farm in Ravelsbach, in the Weinviertel, in 2010. He will return to Schwaz in Tyrol in July 2020. As of 2023, he is 75 years old.
