YASMINE BLEETH looked unrecognizable as she walked her dog in Los Angeles.

The former Baywatch star has rarely been seen in public since quitting acting two decades ago.

In new photos, Yasmine, 54, was spotted makeup free, as she walked alongside the dog.

The former model kept her attire simple, opting for a black jacket, comfortable pink baggy pants and slippers.

She looked a little groggy, and did not smile for the cameras in what are said to be her first public photos in two years.

Yasmine was a TV superstar in the 90s, thanks to her part as Caroline Holden in Baywatch, which also starred Pamela Anderson and David Hasselhoff.

She was one of Hollywood's hottest stars, and turned a guest appearance on the show into a three-season stint.

She filmed 72 episodes in total over the space of five years, as well as featuring in several spin-off films and TV shows.

Her star turn on the show helped to launch her modelling career, and even led the actress to release her own line of swimwear.

Yasmine even dated Matthew Perry and Don Johnson at the height of their fame.

And while she continued to act, with roles in shows such as Nash Bridges and Titans, her behavior behind the scenes did damage to her career.

The actress' career fell apart due to substance abuse issues, with studios and directors blacklisting her because she was considered challenging to work with.

At the height of her addiction, Yasmine stated her cocaine habit was so severe she went without sleep for five days at a time.

She eventually checked into the Malibu-based Promises Rehabilitation Clinic to overcome cocaine dependence.

However, she relapsed after leaving the clinic and ended up getting arrested in 2001 after police found cocaine in her car following a crash.

Yasmine - who hasn't acted since 2003 - admitted her guilt in court through a plea bargain and was sentenced to two years probation, and 100 hours of community service.

While she was in rehab, she met strip club owner Paul Cerrito and the pair tied the knot in August 2002.

The happy couple now split their time between Los Angeles and Scottsdale, Arizona.

In 2013, Yasmine remarked: "Consciously trying to stay off drugs is now part of my life and always will be."
