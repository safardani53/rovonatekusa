But hours later his alleged affair with ex-TV anchor and helicopter pilot Sanchez was revealed after the explicit texts were reportedly obtained by  The National Enquirer.

The mag said it had seen a “cache of selfies...so shocking that we dare not print them”.

Bezos was said to have issued his joint divorce statement just 48 hours after his representatives had been called by the Enquirer about its story.

The magazine is an ally of President Donald Trump — a long-time enemy of Bezos, who owns the Washington Post — fuelling speculation that the story was politically motivated.

Last night the Enquirer featured pictures of Bezos and Sanchez at Santa Monica airport on October 30, where it says they took a ride in her personal helicopter to hike up Malibu Canyon.

Bezos is said to have had his wedding ring on.

The magazine also snapped them at dinner with two pals at the Capo Restaurant in Santa Monica that night — claiming they flirted openly.

It quoted a source as saying: “She was gazing into his eyes. It did not look like a business dinner. He began whispering in her ear and she was listening and gazing at him.”

The Enquirer claimed that MacKenzie once asked her husband why Sanchez was the only passenger listed on his private jet — but he dismissed it as business related.

Bezos got to know mum-of-three Sanchez through her estranged husband Patrick Whitesell during a bash attended by Hollywood A-listers.

Patrick, 53, is a mega-agent to stars including Matt Damon, Christian Bale, Kevin Costner and Hugh Jackman.

The Enquirer alleged that Bezos first tried to woo Sanchez at the premiere of the 2016 movie Manchester by the Sea.
