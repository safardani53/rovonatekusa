DESTROYED By Dr. Phil in Awesome Interview

August 17, 2024
blog

Farrah Abraham stopped by Dr. Phil on Friday to clear up rumors about her sex tape … but the doctor ended up taking her to task over that and a lot more.

Hammering her in a surprisingly patient tone, Dr. Phil touched on her DUI arrest, and accused the Teen Mom star of not taking responsibility for her actions.

When she bristled at his questioning, he responded:

"You will argue no matter what I said because you have a chip on your shoulder… you’re upset because I ask questions you don’t have good answers for."

 

When Abraham accused him of being negative, or putting her in a negative situation, Phil said she’s just upset that she can’t answer real questions.

Unlike Farrah’s mother, Phil added, "I don’t care if you throw a fit or whether you don’t. I just ask you questions like an adult, and if you get upset, okay."

Farrah Abraham went on to tell Dr. Phil she was sorry she even came on, but that just further played into Phil’s hand, as he ripped the reality star’s attitude.

"You have an incredible sense of entitlement," he observed, matter-of-factly.

Somewhere, most likely on a porn set, James Deen is nodding, knowingly.

The wannabe porn star tweeted about her appearance afterward, "I’m responsible , accountable & free from all the twisted BS you read & see PEACE :)"

Glad to see his message got through.
