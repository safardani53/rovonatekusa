Justin Bieber and Selena Gomez feud explained

July 16, 2024
blog

SPECULATION has been rife about a potential feud between Justin Bieber, as well as his wife Hailey, and his ex Selena Gomez.

The conflict allegedly kicked off between Hailey and Selena over a social media post, with Justin believed to have entered the argument later - here's everything we know.

Justin Bieber and Selena Gomez feud explained

Fans have been wondering about a potential falling out between the Biebers and Selena since Hailey seemed to throw shade at the Only Murders In The Building star in February 2023.

It came after Selena made a series of self-deprecating social media videos, including a TikTok clip in which she said: "I laminated my brows too much."

In what some suggested was a response to this, Kylie Jenner shared a photo of her own eyebrows, with the caption: "This was an accident???"

Kylie went on to share a snap of Hailey's brows, with the two of them known to be friends.

Since then, fans of each star have kicked up a comment war with one another online and rumors have run rampant that there are issues between the pair.

For her part, Hailey made a TikTok comment denying everything, saying: "No shade towards Selena ever and I didn’t see her eyebrow posts!

"You guys are making something out of nothing. This is silly."

However this has done little to quell the speculation.

Suggestions of a row only heated up after details emerged about Justin's involvement, particularly what seemed to be a jab at Selena.

What has Justin Bieber said about Selena Gomez?

Neither Justin nor Selena have addressed the alleged spat, but the Baby singer seemed to wade into the issue indirectly.

First of all on March 13, 2023, he posted a snap on Instagram of him and Hailey cuddling on a boat, with the caption: "Luv u baby."

He gave out favors to friends at his birthday party which seemed to allude to his history with Selena.

Music video directors Alfredo Flores and Cole Bennett, who were both at the lavish event, shared photos of $424 Twojeys lighters they had been given by their host.

The engraving on the lighters read: "I'm so grateful that I didn’t end up with what I thought I wanted."

The gift sparked backlash from Gomez's fans, with some urging Justin to "leave Gomez alone".

However, Justin has said he and Selena have nothing but "respect" for each other.

What has Selena Gomez said about Justin Bieber?

While she hasn't commented on the rumors of a feud, Selena also seemed to reference the drama.

In her first TikTok video since the spat was reported to have begun, she urged fans to "be kinder" and "consider others' mental health".

She also seemed to address the issue in the comments, saying that her "heart has been heavy".

Justin and Selena dated on and off between 2010 and 2018.

He married Hailey just six months after they split up for the final time.
