MRS Hinch fans are flocking to Tesco, desperate to nab her brand-new detergent that’s scanning at just £1 at the till. 

In case you missed it, Mrs Hinch recently bought out a new cleaning range with Lenor - in the scent pink tulip and jasmine.

The range is full of a variety of different products, including in-wash scent boosters, washing pods and fabric conditioner. 

Stocked in many stores including Home Bargains, Wilko and even Aldi, shoppers have been raving about the new cleaning range.

But now it’s the new laundry detergent that has now got people talking and not only are people loving the scent, but they’re going wild for the price too, as it’s been scanning at just £1 at Tesco. 

Many bargain hunters have been lucky enough to nab the cut-price buy, one of which was savvy shopper Mark Watson.
