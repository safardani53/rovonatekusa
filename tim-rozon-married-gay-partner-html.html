Insight into 'SurrealEstate' Star Tim Rozons Married Life withLinzey Govan

July 08, 2024

Canadian actor Tim Rozon's married life with partner Linzey Govan is going strong. Since tying the knot, the pair has welcomed a baby son and shared their family bliss via social media handles.

They Married In 2015

Rozon and his girlfriend married in a church ceremony in Sainte-Agathe-des-Monts, Quebec, Canada, on September 6, 2015.

Rozon donned a black tuxedo for the ceremony, while Govan opted for a white mermaid wedding gown with a hip-length veil. The bride's sister Ashley became the maid of honor and their mutual friend, Michael Lord, became the best man. 

It's not clear when the pair started dating, but Govan's Instagram hints they were already in courtship by the early 2000s. She has pictures with him since 2012.

Rozon and His Wife Have a Son

Rozon and his partner welcomed a son in early 2020.

The duo has not disclosed the details of their son's birth, including his birthday and his name. But they have shared enough pictures of him on their social media to let their followers have baby fever.

On June 21, 2020, Govan posted her son's picture for the first time to wish her husband his first Father's Day. Rozon could be seen looking at a picture book with his son in the monochrome photo. 

Govan also Instagrammed a father-son moment on October 6, 2020. They were spending time together in Cape Spear Lighthouse. In the caption, he shared that his best days were the ones spent with his child. 

His Acting Career and Business Ventures

When he is not spending time with his son, Rozon focuses on his acting career. 

Since getting his breakthrough from the teen drama series, Instant Star in 2004, he has won the Gemini Awards for his guest appearance in Flashpoint. He also got nominated for the Canadian Screen Awards as the best leading actor in Befriend and Betray in 2011. 

He has dabbled in over 30 movies and TV series. Some of his notable projects are Schitt's Creek, Lost Girl, Being Human, Saving Hope, Flashpoint, and Wynonna Earp. 

His role of Doc Holliday, an iconic gunslinger, and gambler, in Wynonna Earp, brought him huge attention and appreciation. The last season of the series ended on April 2021.

After his departure from Wynonna Earp, the Quebec native joined Syfy's new drama series, SurrealEstate. In the new show, he portrays Luke Roman, a real estate agent who specializes in selling haunted homes. The drama will premiere on July 16, 2021. 

Besides acting, the Lost Girl actor is also into business ventures. He co-owns two restaurants in Montreal with Canadian celebrity chef Chuck Hughes — Bremer and Garde Manger.
