Did Zakiyah Realize Paulie Was Targeting Her?

September 20, 2024
blog

Is there hope that the women will realize that there’s an all-male alliance in the house?

The females really are disappearing every single week and it’s crazy that no one seems to have caught on to what’s going on. 

During the HOH competition, Paulie was holding on for dear life because he didn’t want any of the women winning. 

He made his intentions blatantly obvious when he dropped right after Zakiyah.

Poor Zakiyah had to go for a cry with Michelle. 

The two of them were totally blindsided by Da’vonne’s departure. They knew nothing about the plan to get her out and looked silly because they were the only two votes to evict Bridgette. 

As the HOH competition continued, Natalie was determined to win it. It came down to her and Victor. 

She kept trying to get Victor to throw the competition to her, but that wasn’t a chance Victor was willing to take. 

Natalie would no doubt take one of the men out of the game. She knows a lot about the all-male alliance because James has been feeding her information about it. 

In fact, it’s surprising that Paulie hasn’t decided to get rid of her yet. 

In the end, Victor was crowned HOH.

Zakiyah and Michelle disappeared into another room to debate what’s really going on in the house. 

Paulie managed to get Zakiyah to speak to him, but she made it clear she knew what his plan was. 

Paulie told her that everything was fine and that she wouldn’t be leaving the house. 

She lied that she believed him and went about her day. 

Paulie told Victor that they should target Michelle instead of Zakiyah.
