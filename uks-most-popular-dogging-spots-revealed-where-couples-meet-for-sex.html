Yorkshire revealed as the most popular place for dogging in the UK

April 04, 2024
blog

YORKSHIRE has been named as the most popular spot for dogging in the country.

God's Own Country has 291 sites where couples meet for sex in semi-secluded locations such as car parks or country parks.

Locations include a water park in Wakefield, a swimming bath in Hull and a track next to the runway of Doncaster Sheffield Airport.

Yorkshire beat its old rival Lancashire, which has 236 dogging areas, according to website Swinging Heaven.

In the south, Kent has 228 dogging spots, while Essex has 203.

The term dogging came from the idea men and women would pretend to be "just taking the dog for a walk" as a cover for nipping out for in-car sexual encounters with strangers.
