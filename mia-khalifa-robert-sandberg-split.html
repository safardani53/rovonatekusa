Why did Mia Khalifa and her husband break up?

On July 21, 2021, former adult actress Mia Khalifa made an announcement about her relationship with her husband.

She called off her wedding celebration to Robert Sandberg despite the pair trying their "absolute hardest".

The media personality had previously delayed her June 2020 wedding due to the Covid pandemic, but then canceled the reception for good.

According to Republic World, Mia and Robert legally got married at their home in 2020 , and had just celebrated their first wedding anniversary.
