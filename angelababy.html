Angelababy Biography

February 06, 2024
blog

In 2008, Angelababy began her romantic love affair with Huang Xiaoming. She dated her as her boyfriend for about six years. Huang Xiaoming is also an actor, model, and singer from China. They got married on the 27th of May 2015.

Angelababy grew up in Shanghai till she was 12 and then she moved to Hong Kong and then she joined Notre Dame College. She also earned a degree from there. While she was in her school days, she was nicknamed Baby though her English name was Angela. At the age of 14, she began her modeling career and began using the name Angelababy. Initially, she was signed to Style International Management. She also appeared in the pseudo-model scene. She is also an actress and has been active as an actress since 2007. She made her acting debut in 2007 by starring the role of Tak Nga in the movie Trivial Matters.  Two years later she starred in the movie Short of Love. She also has a great voice and her debut single Beauty Survivor was released in 2010. She has been earning a lucrative amount of salary and her net worth is said to be around $40.
