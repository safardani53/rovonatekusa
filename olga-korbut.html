Who is Olga Korbut:

Olga Korbut is a famous Gymnast. She was born on May 16, 1955 and her birthplace is Grodno, Belarus. Olga is also well known as, Soviet gymnast who won Olympic gold medals at the 1972 Munich and the 1976 Montreal Olympic games, for the Team, Balance Beam, and Floor Exercise events.

Olga is originated from Belarus. She and Amanda Borden are both gymnasts who went on to win Olympic gold medals.
