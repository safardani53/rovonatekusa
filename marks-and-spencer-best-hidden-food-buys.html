AN M&S superfan has revealed the 20 hidden gems that she swears by - so if you are looking to upgrade your food shop, you’ve come to the right place.

Particularly if you haven’t shopped in M&S before, you’ll be stunned when you see these fabulous goodies.

From the best dessert, the most perfect pasta, and the ultimate biscuit, here’s the buys you’ll need to get your hands on when you next go shopping.

Showing off the brilliant buys on social media, a foodie from Scotland said: “20 hidden gems & classics at Marks & Spencers.”

The food fan then showed off her favourite M&S finds, as she started off strong with a pack of five white chocolate cookies and said: “An absolute classic and a must grab.”

Not only this, but the foodie also raved about the ready to bake, frozen milk chocolate chunk cookies, as well as the all butter triple belgian chocolate cookies, as she wrote: “Frozen baked good heaven.”

If you’ve got a sweet tooth, you’ll need to check out the ready to bake Portuguese custard tarts, as the M&S shopper wrote: “Just spotted today, will probs eat all six tonight.”

But it’s not all food - as the savvy shopper also showed off the M&S fabric conditioner, as she penned: “Does it smell like Le Labo? I’ll let you know.”

Following on from this, the foodie recommended the Fiorelli pasta, as she claimed: “Love this pasta, it has little pockets for sauce and cheese.”

When it came to the viral cotton candy grapes, the M&S fan simply said: “Perfection.”

In terms of savoury food, the foodie recommended the wholegrain and edamame pack, as she added: “The ultimate crunch for a salad.”

Not only this, but we then saw the M&S fan’s trolley, complete with beef strips, a vegetable stir-fry mix, fine egg noodles and Itsu ramen broth.

To this, the foodie wrote: “Such a good deal for stir fry or ramen.”

If you haven’t seen the viral fresh garlic bread, you’re missing out, as the M&S shopper explained: “The most garlic of garlic bread.”

She also loves the sesame pretzel thins, as she explained: “Crunchy deliciousness and buy hummus.”

If you haven’t managed to get your hands on the Serrano ham and manchego rollitos, you’ll need to quick, as the shopper advised: “Buy for a dinner party or just for an afternoon snack.” 

When it comes to dessert, the foodie recommended the Stonebaked s’mores pizza, as she explained: “Didn’t buy but had to flag to the world.”

For a party snack, she swore by the crispy mac ‘n’ cheese bites, as she added: “A guilty pleasure, excellent with sweet chilli sauce.”

For a ‘cute’ treat for kids, the foodie spotted the unicorn steamed buns and claimed: “Too cute to not try.”

For those that love strong flavours in their meals, the foodie showed off the garlic and herb frozen butter, as she continued: “They have so many options, this is genius!”

The frozen aisle proved to be a winner yet again, as the foodie spotted the crunchy peanut bars and wrote: “The Bounty coconut version is better but the best after dinner treat.”

If you’re a fan of cookies, you’ll need to check out the epic range at M&S, as the foodie showed off the £2.25 double chocolate, triple chocolate, salted caramel and white chocolate cookies and claimed: “Buttery and delicious, they have a flavour for everyone”. 

Not only this, but she also claimed that the raspberry cream sandwich fingers are the “ultimate tea biscuit.” 

Finally, another cupboard staple that you’ll need to nab, according to this foodie, is the salted caramel sauce, as she simply concluded: “No words.” 

The TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @girlsaroundscotland, has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed 88,200 views. 

Social media users were stunned at the M&S buys and were eager to express this in the comments. 

One person said: “The freezer section at M&S is NEXT LEVEL.” 

To this, the foodie responded: “Unreal isn’t it.” 

Another added: “Love M&S food and they have many good deals.”
