Aldi confirms it has 'no plans' to bring back discontinued popular fan favorite after customers beg

September 26, 2024
blog

ALDI has confirmed that a popular snack has been discontinued for good.

Shoppers started noticing that the popular Clancys Cheddar & Jalapeno popcorn has been missing from shelves for quite some time.

Clancy's is a private-label brand from Aldi and makes potato chips, popcorn, pretzels, nuts, and more.

However, it was the Cheddar & Jalapeno popcorn that fans loved.

The snack combined salty, cheesy, and spicy flavors that customers enjoyed.

One Twitter user wrote to the large supermarket chain asking if it was going to bring the favorite snack back.

The company replied offering some hope: “No plans just yet! But we'll be sure to let our team know you miss it!”

While that flavor may be gone for good, Aldi is still selling others including Clancy's White Cheddar Cheese Popcorn and Movie Theater Butter Microwave Popcorn.
