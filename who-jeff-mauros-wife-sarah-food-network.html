Who is Jeff Mauro's wife?

March 29, 2024
blog

FOOD Network star, Jeff Mauro, is known for being a co-host on the popular TV series, The Kitchen.

The 44-year-old foodie has had his wife, Sarah, by his side since day one of his rise to fame.

Who is Jeff Mauro's wife?

Sarah Mauro is a former nurse and wife of Food Network star, Jeff Mauro.

The pair met while Jeff was a freshman at Bradley University and have been "inseparable" ever since, according to The Cinemaholic.

The cookbook author and TV personality often praise his wife on social media.

On September 17, 2022, he took to Instagram to post a throwback picture of their wedding, captioned: "17 years today. Lucky to spend a lifetime with the absolute best in the business."

Do Jeff and Sarah have children?

After dating for over 5 years, the pair officially tied the knot in 2005.

Jeff and Sarah currently live in Chicago with their son, Lorenzo, who was born in 2009.

Lorenzo has an affinity for music and his mother often posts videos of him singing and playing the drums on stage at various events.

The family of three has two dogs and is often seen traveling and sharing silly pictures together for social media to enjoy.

Who is Jeff Mauro?

Jeff Mauro rose in popularity after winning Season 7 of Food Network Star.

He is known for his incredible humor and passion for sandwiches.

Jeff is the host of the Emmy-nominated series Sandwich King, as well as a co-host on the Network's popular show, The Kitchen. 

In 2021, the foodie started hosting Kitchen Crash, which premiered its second season in July 2022.

His cookbook - Come On Over: 111 Fantastic Recipes for the Family That Cooks, Eats, and Laughs Together - was published last year.

The restaurant owner has a net worth of $2.3million, according to Famous Chefs.
