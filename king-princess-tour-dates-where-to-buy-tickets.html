King Princess: Tour dates and where to buy tickets

September 03, 2024
blog

KING Princess is heading back on tour for her soon-to-be-released sophomore album.

In 2019 King Princess went on tour for her debut studio album, Cheap Queen.

Where can I buy tickets?

King Princess, whose real name is Mikaela Straus, announced the tour on her Instagram on March 18, 2022.

Tickets for the tour are available to purchase now through her official website at kingprincessmusic.com.

There are some other official ticket sellers for The Hold On Baby Tour, which can be found on her website.

For the Los Angeles show, for example, fans can purchase tickets on the AXS website.

Tickets for the Los Angeles show start at $19.50 and go upwards to $50.

King Princess wrote: "Quick announcement. My album is called Hold On Baby and I’m hand delivering her to your city with special guests @dorajar_ and @stpanther."

She also thanked her girlfriend Quinn Wilson who has worked on some of King Princess' projects in the past.

"Thank you to my wife & creative countess @quinnwilsonn & designer @weiprior / @888.cct."

What are the tour dates?

The tour will begin in Vancouver, British Columbia, on July 5, 2022.

Below are some of the cities King Princess will perform in on tour.

  • Seattle, WA
  • Forest Grove, OR
  • Denver, CO
  • Salt Lake City, UT
  • Berkeley, CA
  • Los Angeles, CA
  • San Diego, CA
  • Austin, TX
  • Houston, TX
  • Charlotte, NC
  • Philadelphia, PA
  • New York, NY
  • Boston, MA

The tour will close on October 15, 2022, in the city of St. Paul, MN.

King Princess will also be performing at the Lollapalooza music festival in July.

Is King Princess releasing new music?

Ahead of the tour KP released the song For My Friends and a music video for the single.

She has confirmed on her Instagram that her album is coming in 2022 and is called Hold On Baby.

KP is signed to Mark Ronson's Zelig Records and often features the musician on social media.

She has also posted the title tracks to her new album on her social media.

King Princess has asked her followership for help in deciding what the next single of her album should be.
