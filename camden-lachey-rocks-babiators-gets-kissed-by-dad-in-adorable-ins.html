“One of the greatest men I know… there’s nothing you can’t do, Baby. You are an amazing Friend, Husband and Daddy. We Love you forever and ever. Happy Father’s Day!”

Nick and Vanessa posed with Camden for the May 2014 issue of Parents, telling the magazine that a sibling for the little guy may soon be on the way.

“We definitely want more kids,” Nick said. “You don’t want kids to have to share the same group of friends. But you don’t want them so far apart that they have nothing in common.”
