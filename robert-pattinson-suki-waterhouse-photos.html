Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse have been dating since 2018. See their best photos here.

Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse began dating sometime in the summer of 2018. They were first spotted engaging in PDA during a late-night stroll in July and were said to be a full-on couple by August. The very private couple spends most of their time in London, where they both have homes — when they’re not working in Hollywood or in other parts of the globe.

Before Suki, Robert had a number of high-profile relationships, including long-term romances with singer FKA Twigs and his Twilight costar, Kristen Stewart. As for Suki, she too was involved in a few high-profile relationships before she set her sights on Robert. The model previously dated Miles Kane, the lead singer of the indie band Last Shadow Puppets, and actors Bradley Cooper, James Marsden, Nat Wolff, Richard Madden, and Diego Luna.
