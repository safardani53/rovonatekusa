Trevor Noah tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Trevor Noah's tour are on sale now and can be purchased in several ways.

Fans can either purchase them directly on Noah's website or through Ticketmaster.

Ticket prices range between $60 and $80, but vary by location.

Who is Trevor Noah?

Born February 20, 1984, Noah, 38, is known as a South African comedian, television host, actor, and political commentator.

He is famously known for his role on The Daily Show and has also released several Netflix specials.

In June 2022, he made headlines after it was revealed that he was being eyed by CBS to take over James Corden's spot on The Late Late Show after he announced he would be leaving in 2023.

A source told The Sun: "Most of the crew found out the day before everyone else that James was leaving, and everyone will be sad to see him go.

"The main name floating around behind the scenes at the moment is Trevor Noah; he'd be perfect and has more than enough experience.
