Katie Price reveals 29 stone son Harvey is launching his own 7XL clothing range so he has a job

June 27, 2024
blog

KATIE Price's 29 stone son Harvey is set to launch an 7XL clothing range so he has his own income, The Sun Online can exclusively reveal.

Harvey, 18, is partially blind and suffers from a range of complex disabilities, including Prader-Willi syndrome.

Suffers of the genetic condition often experience a constant craving for food, which - like in Harvey's case - leads to excessive weight gain.

Harvey now tips the scales at just under 30 stone but Katie is hoping to turn this into a positive to help benefit his future.

In an exclusive clip from Katie's latest YouTube video, the star can be seen chatting to founder of Big Clothing 4 U, Ben Pearson.

The online company specialises in affordable clothes for larger men and Ben is hoping to collaborate with Harvey.

Katie tells Ben in the clip: "He deserves like everyone else in life to have a chance.

"I want him to have a clothing range out...because it will be for him, and that's why I've come to you.

"I want you and him to do it. It's nothing to do with me. Obviously I'll be the one behind it promoting it with him...."

Ben then chips in to mention Katie's ongoing fight to get Harvey's Law passed by Parliament.

If successful, it will become illegal to troll or abuse other people online.

Ben will also be working with Katie on the campaign, making t-shirts that read 'online trolling should be illegal'.

Katie is planning to give a press conference about the campaign - and will be supported by Ben.

Agreeing with Ben, Katie continues: "So I want you and Harvey to do something together, as that's long-term for him and he's got an income himself."

Katie gave birth to Harvey in May 2002 following a brief relationship with now-retired footballer Dwight Yorke.

At just two months old, it was discovered that Harvey was partially blind. He was then diagnosed with Prader-Willi syndrome.

Harvey is also on the autistic spectrum.
