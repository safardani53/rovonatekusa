Robin Daniel Skinner, known professionally as Cavetown, is an English singer-songwriter, record producer, and YouTuber. His style blends elements of indie rock, indie pop and bedroom pop with mellow, gentle ukulele ballads. As of February 2022, he had amassed over 8.2 million monthly streamers on Spotify. His YouTube channel, which he began in November 2012, sits at 1.79 million subscribers and 321 million video views as of December 2021. Skinner released his fourth studio album, Sleepyhead, in March 2020.
