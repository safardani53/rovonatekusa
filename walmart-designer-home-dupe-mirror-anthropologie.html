Walmart fans rush to buy designer home dupe selling for $500 less than original - and it's a social

June 25, 2024

BARGAIN shoppers can grab an almost identical dupe of a high-end brand's most popular item at a fraction of the price.

Anthropologie's most popular item can now be bought at Walmart for $65.

Walmart Better Homes & Gardens mirror is supposed to be a perfect dupe for the Gleaming Primrose Mirror from Anthropologie that retails at $548.

The Anthropologie best seller has been making its way around social media, seen with celebrities like Shay Mitchell and Joanna Gaines.

The Walmart mirror costs $65 and is described by the brand to be perfect for the living room, dining room, bedroom, and more.

The mega-retailer highlighted its leaflet scrolls and flower detailing to create its classic décor style.

Described as "luxurious and affordable," the home décor measures at 20 feet by 30 feet and came with a gold finish.

"This mirror is very pretty and nicely made. It is a heavy mirror and the mirror glass is a nice thick mirror. I was going to hang this in my upstairs hall bathroom, but decided to hang it in my bedroom instead," said one reviewer.

The Walmart dupe is a great way to get the antique feeling of the Gleaming Primrose Mirror, but they are a few things to keep in mind.

The original Anthropologie mirror comes in four colors including black, gold, white, and silver. In contrast, the Walmart mirror only comes in gold.

Most read in Money

Julie Ramhold, a consumer analyst with DealNews.com, recommended to Best Life Online, that those who are particularly handy should spray paint a different finish if gold is not their preference.

"And considering the much higher price at Anthropologie, picking up some cans of high-quality spray paint and primer, as well as some painter's tape to give the Walmart mirror a new look, is still going to be much cheaper than buying even the smallest mirror at Anthropologie."

Also, those who had a specific size in mind must know that the Better Homes & Gardens mirror is much smaller than the Anthropologie original which starts at 39.25 feet by 39 feet.

If the Walmart mirror sounds like the right choice for you, consider picking it up from your local store.

"For anyone interested in the Walmart dupe, it's best to buy in-store and select the best one, as the quality may vary from each mirror, and the likelihood of it breaking during shipping is high," Jeanel Alvarado, founder and CEO of Retail Boss, recommends.
