Juwan Johnsons Wife Is the Biggest Supporter in His Life

August 15, 2024
blog

Juwan Johnson's wife Chanen Raygoza is undoubtedly one of his biggest supporters.

In October 2020, she was visibly overwhelmed with happiness after learning that her husband was set to play his first NFL game for the New Orleans Saints.

As seen in a viral clip, the wide receiver set up his phone in front of his wife, who was busy on her phone. As he revealed the news to her, she expressed her disbelief at first. 

Upon realizing it was the truth, she hugged her spouse, expressing her contentment over his success. 

The former Oregon player's debut for the Saints came after spending the first six weeks of the season on the practice squad.

The official announcement came a day before his debut match at the weekend.

The NFL player got his big break after the Saints activated him from the practice squad following injuries to several key players, including Emmanuel Sanders and Michael Thomas.

The football player capped off his first match in 19% of offensive snaps and 27% of special teams snaps against the Carolina Panthers.

Juwan Johnson's Wife Supports His Career 

Johnson's signing with the Saints came soon after being projected as a potential late-round draft pick.

The player was offered a three-year deal worth $85,000 with $10,000 in signing bonus.

A former grad transfer from Penn State University, he played eight games as a senior in 2019.

His limited appearance was a result of a lower leg injury. However, Johnson returned to the team against the Duck's on-field encounter against Colorado on October 11.

In his final game for the Oregon-based college team, the wide receiver secured the final first down needed to win the Rose Bowl in January 2020.

He led his team in receiving the Rose Bowl with five receptions for sixty-six yards, including the final twenty-eight-yard reception helping his team win with 28-27.

In 2019, the athlete had secured a number of pivotal plays for his team, including two receptions on the final drive against Washington State that saw the Ducks clinch a last-second field goal which Johnson contributed to set up.

During his maiden spell at Oregon, the player secured thirty receptions for four hundred seventy-six yards and four touchdowns.

Johnson capped off his illustrious college career with one-hundred-eleven receptions for fifteen-hundred-ninety yards and six touchdowns.

And in his career, one person who has been by his side through ups and downs is his spouse.

In an Instagram post dated December 17, 2021, Johnson posted a picture with his wife. In the caption, he alluded that his partner had remained by his side in his professional endeavors.

Juwan Johnson's Wedding with Wife

Johnson and his wife reportedly first met in 2016 at Penn State, where Raygoza was a gymnast.

After dating her for a long time, the professional footballer proposed to his wife in January 2020.

He took to Instagram to announce the news of his engagement, posting a picture with Raygoza where she was spotted with a shiny white ring on her finger.

"Forever with you sounds good to me," Johnson captioned the photo.

Juwan Johnson and his wife during their private wedding hosted at a Las Vegas Hotel. (Photo: YouTube)

The following month, on February 15, the couple tied the knot during a small ceremony hosted at the Las Vegas Strip.

The venue for the wedding included a decked-out suite and a hotel pool with a slide that went through a fish tank.

However, the bride and groom did not invite any family members or friends to witness them getting married.

The couple documented their special day, and a week after their nuptials released the wedding video on their YouTube channel.

Likewise, Johnson also posted the wedding video followed by a lengthy caption on his Instagram.
