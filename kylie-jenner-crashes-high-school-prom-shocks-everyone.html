Kylie Jenner Crashes High School Prom, Shocks Everyone

July 11, 2024
blog

Kylie Jenner is all about giving back these days. 

That’s about all we can muster after the Keeping Up with the Kardashian star’s latest act of goodwill. The first act was ditching her much older boyfriend, Tyga, who was pretty much only with her to cash in on the Kardashian brand. 

The 19-year-old reality TV star accompanied Albert Ochoa to his High School prom in Sacramento on Saturday night. As you can probably expect, the other kids in attendance went crazy at the prospect of having one of most talked about young celebrities in attendance. 

Kyle took her pal, Jordyn Woods along for the momentous occasion and it sure looks like the event was a roaring success with everyone who went. 

Ochoa’s sister posted a video on Twitter of Kylie and her brother hugging, as everyone down below could only look on in awe.
