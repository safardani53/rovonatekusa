David Archuleta to Guest Star on iCarly

blog

From American Idol to iCarly.

David Archuleta is set to make his actint debut on the Nickelodeon sitcom, as the season seven runner-up will portray himself in an episode that will air in the spring.

The show itself focuses on a high school student, played by Miranda Cosgrove, who runs a video blog with her friends. This is where Archuleta will come in.

In the episode titled “iRocked the Vote,” Archuleta apears on a show called America Sings. He enjoys an increase in votes after winning support in an iCarly webcast.

The guest-starring stint is part of the singer’s promotional plans for his debut album, which comes out on November 11.
