Man Utd new 2021-22 away kit 'leaked online' with striking resemblance to iconic retro white and blu

March 29, 2024
blog

MANCHESTER UNITED'S new away kit for the 2021-22 has seemingly been leaked online, with fans quickly falling in love.

Renowned kit-leak website Footy Headlines have shared what they believe to be United's away strip for next season.

The top will see new sponsor TeamViewer across the front for the first time from January 2022.

But all eyes are instead locked on the iconic, retro colour scheme.

The top bears blue and white zig-zags across the front and even features adidas' old 'Trefoil' logo, rather than the modern day three stripes.

And fans have been left thrilled with the throwback jersey - most-famously worn by Ryan Giggs in his debut season.

United wore the eerily-similar blue and white kit between 1990-91 and 1991-92 just as the Welsh winger was breaking into the first-team.

Other big names to wear the shirt include Paul Ince, Mark Hughes, Steve Bruce, Gary Pallister, Denis Irwin and Bryan Robson.

Man Utd won the League Cup in 1991-92 wearing the blue and white strip - as they edged past Nottingham Forest.

Fans quickly took to social media to show the new kit some love.
