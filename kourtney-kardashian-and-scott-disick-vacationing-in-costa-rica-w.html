Vacationing in Costa Rica WITHOUT Sofia Richie!

April 13, 2024
blog

Another day, another Kardashian family vacation. 

But this trip may be, just possibly, the most interesting yet. 

Our thoughts go out to Sofia Richie, who HAS to be experiencing the worst FOMO right now and probably a little bit of jealousy, too. 

Ok, if she is actually a legitimate human she is probably feeling a lot of jealousy right now because her BF Scott Disick is currently vacationing with baby mama and ex GF Kourtney Kardashian in Costa Rica…

While she was left out to dry! 

Well technically she is busy working in NYC, but the situation still undeniably sucks for the model. 

For those Keeping Up with the Kardashian fans who are already convinced that Kourtney and Scott’s killer co-parenting skills are just a ploy to hide their passionate love for each other, we hear you.

Kourtney and Scott are on yet another vacation (it’s all they do, I swear) with their kids.

The former couple took Mason, Penelope, and Reign to hang out with cousins North, Saint, and Chicago — and this time it involved getting their passports stamped.

Yes, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West are also on the family getaway. 

"They are all filming for the show," a source told People.

"They have a lot of ocean and rainforest activities planned." 

On top of this already odd news, the families are planning to soak up the Costa Rica sun for two whole weeks! 

That’s a lot of time for Kourtney and Scott to fall back in love, just saying.

And if that’s not their plan, then that’s a lot of time to spend with an ex. (hard pass)

On Thursday Kourt shared a video to her Instagram Story that showed off her beautiful view featuring palm trees and a long infinity pool. 

And although this isn’t the first time the cordial exes have vacationed together, the timing is questionable.

This vacay comes after multiple engagement rumors involving Disick, 36 and Richie, 20.

Also, remember Kourtney and Disick’s trip to Bali where they met with a healer who basically said Kourtney and Scott were soulmates and are meant to be together? 

"Scott came in, and the guy was basically saying that in a past life that we were together, and that we’re soul mates," she explained during a testimonial on KUWTK. 

"So he was like ‘You have to decide if you want to be together or not be together.’"

Maybe this intimate trip is for them to finally decide?

Or in a more boring world, and probably the right one, maybe the two just tagged along with Kimye because it’s summer and what else do they have going on?

This trip marks six weeks after Kim and Kanye welcomed their fourth child together, Psalm, via surrogate.

The KKW Beauty mogul shared the photo of her newborn on May 17, where she officially revealed to the world his rather unique name.

"Beautiful Mother’s Day with the arrival of our fourth child," Kanye wrote to Kim in a text message that she later shared to social media. 

"We are blessed beyond measure. We have everything we need," read the message. 

Matriarch Kris Jenner explained the meaning behind the name Psalm to all of us on Entertainment Tonight.

Although I don’t think it takes a genius to figure that one out, especially if you are aware of Kanye’s recent and highly publicized side gig — his Sunday church service. 

"The inspiration was the Book of Psalms in the Bible," Kris revealed. 

"I think it’s just a wonderful way to celebrate how they feel."

"And he’s such a blessing, so it’s perfect," said Kris.

We’re sure the fam is enjoying their time together and giving baby Psalm all the love.

And as for Kourtney, Scott, and Sofia — well they’re apparently cool with one another. 

They’ve gone on vacations together in the past and even successfully snapped one of the most iconic IG photos ever. 

But we can’t help but doubt their current situation, I mean it’s hard to imagine that no one will end up feeling betrayed in this 3-way scenario. 

As Kourtney and Scott said in a recent video which they shared to Poosh, "I couldn’t imagine raising three children with somebody I couldn’t speak to every day."

"I think other people think if they were in [our] position, they couldn’t do it," Scott said. 

Yep, you’ve got that right!

But then again, we couldn’t imagine what it is like to be in a Kardashian’s position.
