How To Pick the Right Cushion Foundation

Skin tone match

Select a cushion foundation shade closest to your natural skin tone for the best results. Try products in-store to determine what shade is right for you, or read reviews online to get an idea of how the product looks on different skin tones.

Coverage

Decide how much coverage you want from your cushion foundation. Opt for a sheer coverage product if you prefer something light and natural looking. For more coverage, choose a product that provides medium to full coverage.

Finish

Choose the finish of your cushion foundation based on the look you want — matte for an oil-free finish, satin for a luminous look, or dewy for a natural glow.

SPF protection

Cushion foundations with built-in SPF are available — look out for products with SPF 30 or higher for protection against UVB and UVA rays.

Ingredients

Select a cushion foundation that contains nourishing ingredients such as vitamins and antioxidants to help keep skin healthy and promote a radiant complexion.

Hydration

If you have dry skin, opt for a cushion foundation that provides hydration to keep your skin hydrated and soft throughout the day. Look out for formulas enriched with hyaluronic acid or similar nourishing ingredients to lock in moisture and keep your skin looking its best.

Ease of use

Consider how easy it is to use the product — you want something quick and easy to apply without leaving unsightly streaks or patches. Read reviews online to understand how easily others have applied the product before purchasing.

Lasting power

Choose a cushion foundation with long-wearing results so you don't need to keep reapplying throughout the day — products with built-in primer or setting powder can help with this, too!

Compatibility with other makeup

Check that the cushion foundation is compatible with other products in your makeup bag before purchasing. Some formulas may not blend well, so checking this before committing to buying is important.
