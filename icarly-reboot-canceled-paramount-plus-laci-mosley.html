THE iCarly reboot has been iCanceled after three seasons on Paramount+.

Series star Laci Mosley revealed the news online, and it was soon confirmed by a network spokesperson.

Laci, who plays former socialite-turned-barista Harper in the reboot of the beloved Nickelodeon series, broke the news on X, the platform formerly known as Twitter.

"It’s canceled babes," she stated simply, after one fan wondered about a Season 4.

Laci continued: "Not a joke. The best people I’ve ever worked with in the business."

She added a personal note to fans of the show.

"Thank you for tuning in to the reboot. Everyone put their deeply kind talented souls into this."

Shortly after Laci shared the news, a spokesperson confirmed it.
