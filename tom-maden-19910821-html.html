Tom Maden is an American actor, perhaps best known for his recurring role as Jake Fitzgerald in the anthology killer MTV's Scream series, from 2015 to 2016. Prior to acting, Maden also starred in the third season of the Discovery Kids Endurance reality TV series. Other major credits in Maden's career include leading roles in the online series Lifeline and All Night, as well as many recurring roles in the TV series, such as Parenthood, Make It or Break It, and Famous in Love. Additionally, Maden has starred in a number of films, including The Nanny Express, General Education, and The Ninth Passenger.
