What is Kendall Jenner's net worth and is she richer than Kylie?

July 22, 2024
blog

THE Kardashian-Jenner clan are estimated to have a combined net worth of more than $2billion - but how much is Kendall Jenner worth?

The model and media personality has made an eye-watering amount of money since rising to stardom in 2007 on Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

Why is Kendall Jenner famous?

Kendall Jenner is best known for being a member of the Kardashian family and for appearing in the reality television show, Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

The success of the show led to the creation of multiple spin-off series, before The Kardashians was launched in 2022.

However, Kendall is also an incredibly successful model, having started her career at just 14-years-old, and has featured in campaigns, editorials, and cover shoots by Vogue, Harper's Bazaar and Vanity Fair.

In 2015, Kendall appeared in her very first Victoria's Secret fashion show and has represented global brands such as Adidas, Pepsi and Calvin Klein.

In October 2021, she became the highest-paid model in the world with an income of US$40m.

Kendall is also the brand ambassador of Seventeen magazine - for girls and women aged between 12-19 - and Estee Lauder.

Among her many other ventures, she launched the tequila brand 818 Tequila and partnered with skin-care company Proactiv in 2019.

What is Kendall Jenner's net worth?

According to celebritynetworth.com, Kendall's net worth is $60million as of 2023.

As a result of her reality television appearances, modelling success and countless brand deals, the 27-year-old has built up a remarkable portfolio and earned eye-watering sums of money.

She was named the world's highest paid model by Forbes in 2017, before earning $22m over the following 12 months.

Kendall also earns money from social media posts, where a typical sponsored post will earn her up to $150,000, as well as public appearance fees.

Kendall has also built up a real estate portfolio and made her first acquisition with the purchase of a condo in California for $1.3m.

In October 2017, she picked up a five-bedroom home in Beverly Hills for $8.55 million in the much more secure gated community of Mulholland Estates.

A short career in photography also added to her earnings but numerous business collaborations have really bumped up her cashflow, including the launch of her own mobile app in 2015.

However, she is not even close to reaching the heights of her sister, Kylie Jenner, who has an estimated net worth of $680m.

Who is Kendall Jenner related to?

Kendall shares the same mother and father with her younger sister, Kylie - Kris Jenner and Caitlyn Jenner.

However, she also has eight older half-siblings, as a result of previous marriages of her parents.

From Caitlyn's side of the family, she has three older half-brothers―Burt, Brandon, and Brody Jenner―and one older half-sister Casey Marino

Through Kris's previous marriage to Robert Kardashian, Kylie also has three more older half-sisters in Kourtney Kardashian, Kim Kardashian, and Khloe Kardashian, and an older half-brother, Rob Kardashian.

She is also an aunty to 11 children - Stormi and Aire (Kylie); Mason, Reign and Penelope (Kourtney); True and Tatum (Khloe); and North, Chicago, Psalm and Saint (Kim).

Kendall is believed to be single but has previously had long-term relationships with NBA players Blake Griffin, Ben Simmons and Devin Booker.
