Bayern Munich vs PSG: Prediction, odds and betting tips

TWO of Europe's best teams meet in pre-season as they prepare to fight for silverware next term.

They are taking part in the International Champions Cup, which features the likes of Real Madrid, Man Utd, Spurs and other giants.

YOU would be forgiven for thinking this could be next year’s Champions League final.

Bayern Munich are heavy favourites at 8/15 with Paddy Power to win today, most likely because PSG will be without World Cup winner Kylian Mbappe and Neymar.

The Germans are well priced to win and both teams to score at 9/5 with Paddy Power.

But if PSG can get ahead early they should be able to see it out and we fancy PSG HT, PSG FT at 7/1 with Paddy Power.
