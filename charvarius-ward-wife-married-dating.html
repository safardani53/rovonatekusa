While his football career continues to soar, fans have also been curious about the wife of Charvarius Ward.

In this article, we’ll look into Ward’s personal life, exploring his marital status, relationship, and inspiring life story.

Charvarius Ward is a talented American football cornerback who currently plays for the San Francisco 49ers of the NFL.

Born on May 16, 1996, in McComb, Mississippi, Ward’s journey to the NFL has been nothing short of remarkable.

Ward’s football career began to take shape during his senior year at McComb High School.

His collegiate journey saw him start at Hinds Community College, later transferring to Middle Tennessee State.

Ward signed with the Dallas Cowboys in 2018. However, he was traded to the Kansas City Chiefs at the end of the 2018 preseason.

After culminating in a Super Bowl victory in Super Bowl LIV with the Chiefs, Ward embarked on a new chapter with the San Francisco 49ers in 2022.

Is Charvarius Ward Married? Dating And Relationship

As the 49ers cornerback continues to excel in his career, fans and admirers delve more into his personal life. They’re eager to know Charvarius Ward’s wife.

As of now, Ward is not married. However, he is in a relationship with his beautiful girlfriend, Monique.

Meet Charvarius Ward Girlfriend, Monique

Although the player is mostly silent about his private life, Monique is the name that sticks out in relation to his dating life.

Meanwhile, very little public information on the internet about the NFL player’s partner is available.

Monique, like Ward, values her privacy and does not share much about her personal life on social media either.

As a result, it’s not known how or when Charvarius and Monique met and what formed the basis of their relationship.

Moreover, the limited online presence of the couple makes it even harder to track the relationship timeline.

However, Monique occasionally shares glimpses of her life with the NFL star on Instagram. It includes a few vacation videos featuring the couple.

Their relationship took a significant turn in November 2022 when Monique gave birth to their daughter, Amani Joi Ward.

The arrival of their baby girl was a joyous occasion, but it came with its share of challenges.

Parenthood Amidst Adversity

Amani Joi was born on November 17, 2002. As per reports, she was born prematurely, five weeks before her due date.

Ward, dedicated not only to his football career but also to his family, rushed to Memphis from Colorado Springs when Monique went into labor.

However, their newborn daughter faced complications and had to be placed in the neonatal intensive care unit (NICU), hooked to tubes and monitors.

Amani Joi finally came home from the hospital in December, ending a wrenching, two-week ordeal for the parents.

In April 2023, Charvarius Ward revealed that Amani Joi had undergone open-heart surgery, a challenging and emotional experience for any parent.

He took to social media to express his gratitude for the outpouring of support from his followers and fans during this trying time.

He offered an update on Amani’s condition, stating she was on the “road to recovery.”
