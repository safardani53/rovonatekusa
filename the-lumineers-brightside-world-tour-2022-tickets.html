Who are The Lumineers?

Wesley Schultz and Jeremiah Fraites came together in 2005 to start creating music.

In 2010, they were joined by cellist and vocalist Neyla Pekarek, who remained with the band until 2018.

Although they began playing together in the early 2000s, The Lumineers self-titled debut album didn't come out until 2012.

They released their second album, Cleopatra, in 2016; it debuted as number one on the Billboard 200 chart.

On September 13, 2019, their third album, III, was released, debuting as number two.

Brightside, their fourth studio album, came out on January 14, 2022.
