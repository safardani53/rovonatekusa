Taylor Armstrong Having an Affair with Attorney John Bluher

February 05, 2024
blog

Taylor Armstrong is having an affair with her attorney John Bluher, according to a source close to the couple.

Bluher, 54, who is married with three children, has been helping the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star with a lawsuit she is involved in with MyMedicalRecords.com.

According to the source, who spoke to RadarOnline, “John traveled with Taylor while she was doing her book tour and the two of them hit it off, in more ways than one.”

“She’s been seeing him for almost two months and has confided in a handful of friends about the affair,” says the insider.

John’s, who is currently married to Stephanie Bluher, is from Parker, Colorado, but spends the marjority of his time with Taylor in California now, the source continues.

“It’s a bit of a complicated situation. John is still technically married, even though he no longer lives with his wife in Colorado.”

The source says that although she has made great progress since her husband Russell Armstong‘s suicide six months ago, friends feel Taylor is still in a vulnerable position, and they are not fans of John.

RadarOnline was not able to reach Taylor or John for comment, but when they reached John’s wife Stephanie by phone, she said, “I don’t have anything to say to you. You can take it up with my husband.”

Side-note: In the season finale of RHOBH, the women accuse Lisa Vanderpump of selling info to her friend at RadarOnline. Lisa is insulted by the incinuation that she would ever sell information, and says she doesn’t need the money. She emphasises several times that she has never “sold” information, however, she is not questioned about, nor denies that she has GIVEN information to the tabloid. Just sayin’.
