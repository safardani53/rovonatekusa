Katy Perry has revealed the new album cover for “Teenage Dream”. The depiction of the musical artist lying nude on a fluffy pink mound of cotton candy clouds was painted by artist Will Cotton.

It’s Ace Of Base! It’s Cyndi Lauper! It’s like all these colors and more. It’s going to be one of those records that is everybody’s favorite guilty pleasure.”

The first single from the album is titled “California Gurls”. It hit the radio on May 7, reaching #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 in just four weeks.
