NO-ONE wants their wedding night to be blighted by blisters, leaving you limping to the nearest chair when you'd rather be dancing the night away.

With this in mind, Kate Spade has teamed up with Keds to bring brides a range of wedding trainers.

Because, let's be honest, no-one will be able to see what you're wearing under that full-length gown anyway.

With prices starting at £72, they're not the cheapest - but you could definitely wear them again after the big day.

The kicks are bejewelled with pearls, rhinestones and glitter - there's even two platform pairs for the shorties among us.

"The collection is perfect for everything from bridal showers to bridesmaids’ gifts to walking down the aisle to happily ever after," Keds said in a press release.

"Equal parts style and function, these styles are a must for brides-to-be.
