Career

Vikram Kirloskar, a son of the Kirloskar family, was associated with the Kirloskar group, which makes various mechanical items. Kirloskar Brothers Limited, Kirloskar Pneumatic Company Limited, Toyota Kirloskar Motor, Kirloskar Oil Engines Limited, Kirloskar Brothers Limited, and Kirloskar Chillers Private Limited are all part of the Kirloskar Group. He started his Kirloskar Group career as a manufacturing apprentice at Kirloskar Cummins in Pune, where he contributed new innovations and technology to the firm.

He struck a contract with Toyota in the late 1990s and introduced it to India. Vikram formed a partnership with Toyota via his holding and investment firm, Kirloskar Systems Ltd. Initially, Vikrams’ firm did business with Toyota for textiles, but he subsequently decided to get into the automotive market, eventually being engaged in the production of Toyota vehicles in India. Toyota India is owned by Vikram’s firm, which owns 11% of the corporation. His companies, Toyota Motor Corporation, and Kirloskar Group concentrate on Toyota automobile sales and manufacture in India. Toyota Qualis, their first automobile, was introduced in India in 1997.
