TODAY Show host Hoda Kotb has shocked the show's crew after spraying her co-host Jenna Bush Hager with Cheez-Whiz.

The wild off-the-script moment happened when the women were playing an on-air game.

On Friday's show, also known as National Cheese Lover's Day, Hoda, 58, and Jenna, 41, stood with the show's culinary producer, Katie, to play Seize That Cheese!

The object of the game was to answer a cheese-related question correctly and whoever did got to keep the cheese that was brought out for that round.

The player with the most cheese at the end of the game would win a prize.

After a few rounds, there was a tie.

Cheez-Whiz was brought out as the last cheese.

The question was: "What is Chuck E. Cheese's middle name?"

After looking at the three options, Jenna answered correctly with "Entertainment."

When Jenna got the answer correct, Hoda picked up the spray cheese, took the cap off, and shook it.

"No no no!" Jenna screamed. "No no no!"

"No, you won, so yay!" Hoda said, as she aimed the bottle toward Jenna.

"That doesn't mean you spray me with Cheez Whiz," Jenna laughed, as she stuck her hands up.

Hoda put the can down and asked what Jenna won, which was a box of Ritz Crackers.

But, after she received her prize, Hoda picked it back up again.

The Today host sprayed some on her finger, but it wound up spraying and hitting Jenna and the floor.

The crew yelled and laughed off-camera and the women made disgusted faces.

"So, this was a husband. He was outraged after he found out that his wife had been using his toothbrush," Jenna explained.

"She says that she just uses it from time to time," Hoda chimed in. "Don't get all bent out of shape. She doesn't use it daily."

"And sometimes it can be by accident," Jenna said.

"Maybe you confuse the brushes."

"Especially on a trip," Hoda said. "Because you bring one of those travel brushes and you're like, 'Is that yours?'"

"You know what I meant, mouth to mouth," Jenna defended herself.

"Teeth to teeth is like when you don't know how to kiss and you bang your teeth together," Hoda said.
