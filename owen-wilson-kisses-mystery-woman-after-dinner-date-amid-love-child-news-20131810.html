Owen Wilson Kisses Mystery Woman At Dinner Date Amid Love Child News

March 24, 2024
blog

Baby daddy does date night! A rep for Owen Wilson recently confirmed to Us Weekly that he will soon welcome a child with personal trainer Caroline Lindqvist, but that hasn’t stopped the Hollywood hunk from playing the field. 

Wilson, 44, was spotted outside Santa Monica eatery Rustic Canyon on Monday, Oct. 7, passionately smooching a mystery brunette woman after sharing a meal together.

In the recently released photos, the Internship actor looks smitten as he wraps his arms around his new love. Both kept their look casual for the dinner date, with Wilson in a cap, hoodie and sneakers and his mystery gal in an all-black outfit and flip-flops.

The woman was later photographed leaving the actor’s home at approximately 10 a.m. the next morning.

Late last week, a source told Us that though Wilson is not currently dating Lindqvist — whom he’d originally hired as his trainer several years ago — he still plans on helping out any way he can.

“Owen is very involved with the pregnancy,” the source said. “He isn’t in a relationship with Caroline, nor do they plan to be in one, but he is very supportive of everything. They see each other and talk often about what they want for the child.” 

“Owen is very open-minded about everything and has told Caroline he will do whatever he can,” the source added.
