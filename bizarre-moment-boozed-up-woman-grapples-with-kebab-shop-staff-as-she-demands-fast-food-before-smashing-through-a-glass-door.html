THIS is the bizarre moment a boozed-up woman battled with pizza shop staff - before smashing through a glass door.

Shocking footage shows the woman desperately trying to open the door to get her fast food fix last night.

But staff blocked her path before she is shoved to the pavement outside Apache Pizza in Belfast.

The determined woman gets back up and is kicked by another man, believed to be a customer.

She is sent sprawling again after being pushed by a uniformed worker.

Bu amazingly she bounced back up to her feet and launched a third attempt to get into the shop which ended when the glass door smashes.

Stunned student Ryan Hart filmed the incredible footage and posted it on Facebook with the caption: "Someone get this woman a kebab".
