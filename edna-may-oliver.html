Who is Edna May Oliver:

Edna May Oliver is a famous Movie Actress. She was born on November 9, 1883 and her birthplace is Massachusetts. Edna is also well known as, American actress who was well-known for playing character roles in the 1930s in such films as Cimarron (as Mrs. Tracy Wyatt) and Little Women (as Aunt March). She was nominated for an Oscar in the Best Supporting Actress category in 1939.

Edna is originated from United States. She co-starred with Mary Boland in the 1925 Broadway production of The Cradle Snatchers.
