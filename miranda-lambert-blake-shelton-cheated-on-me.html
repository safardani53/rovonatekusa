Miranda Lambert claims she was never unfaithful during her marriage to fellow country star Blake Shelton, of whom she cannot say the same.

Ever since their shocking-not-shocking divorce filing earlier this week, a split that had been spun as amicable is looking less and less so.

Yesterday, a report insinuated that Blake suspected Miranda cheated on him on two occasions, and the loyal husband peaced out as a result.

Country star Chris Young denied an affair or any involvement with her after tabloid rumors linked him to Miranda more than once last year.

Miranda is far from pleased about these stories, which she believes are part of a PR offensive to make her look bad – and Blake look better.

Lambert was never unfaithful during her four-year marriage to Shelton, sources close to her are saying … but The Voice coach is another story.

Her camp strongly suggests Shelton strayed – with a famous country singer no less – and that this is all an unscrupulous attempt to smear her.

TMZ says she’s incensed he accused her of cheating with a country singer (Young), which she says never happened, as does the third party.

The divorce is so bitter, Blake ordered Miranda to clear her animals off of his ranch in Oklahoma the day the judge made the split official.

That, and if you believe her side, he’s been trying to deflect his alleged infidelity (the name Cady Groves comes to mind) with lies about her.
