Lindsay Lohan Is Nude on Instagram &amp; Fans Don't Know What to Think

July 03, 2024

For a very brief time at the beginning of this year, it looked as though Lindsay Lohan might be on the verge of a comeback.

Rather than continuing to chase pipe dreams, such as starring in a live-action remake of Disney’s The Little Mermaid, Linds appeared to have given up on acting in favor of embracing her tabloid trainwreck side.

That might sound depressing, but for LiLo it looked like a step in the right direction.

After all, no one had cared about her acting skills since Mean Girls, and the Disney thing was simply never gonna happen.

Unfortunately, Lindsay’s commitment to laughing along with her haters proved half-hearted.

Lindsay Lohan’s Beach Club wasn’t quite the trashy camp-fest fans had hoped, and the show bizarrely attempted to rebrand Lindsay as a Lisa Vanderpump-esque mogul/mentor type.

It didn’t work; that’s not what viewers wanted; and Beach Club was canceled after its first season.

But that doesn’t mean that Lindsay is done for good.

No, she recently launched a two-pronged campaign to revitalize her career involving both social media nudity and Australian singing competitions.

Linds is a judge on the Aussie version of The Masked Singer, which we imagine is quite the spectacle.

The American version of The Masked Singer was a debacle, and the Down Under iteration likely features some random bushperson dressed like a giant can of Foster’s belting out a drunken rendition of "Waltzing Matilda."

We pray it will soon be available for streaming in the US.

Anyway, Linds might be the only one who comes out of this thing looking good, as fans are raving about the sheer dress she wore on the first night of taping (above).

Though it’s worth noting that a surprising number of people felt she looked like a wax figure, and we’re pretty sure they didn’t mean that as a compliment. 

“You should be in Madame Tussauds,” wrote one fan

“Very Madame Tussauds," another commented.

You get the idea.

Anyway, as you can see, the semi-see-through dress is actually pretty tame by the standards of Lindsay’s latest pics

The response from fans has been decidedly mixed.

So much so, in fact, that Lohan disabled comments on the above pic, which seems to be inspired by the title of Snoop Dogg’s debut album. Look it up.

Lots of folks are suggesting that Lindsay might be starved for attention.

Maybe that’s the case … but maybe she’s just feeling herself.

Whatever the case, we’re guessing she’s happy with the results.
