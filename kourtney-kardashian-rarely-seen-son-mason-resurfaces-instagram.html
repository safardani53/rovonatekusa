KOURTNEY Kardashian has shared a pic of her rarely-seen son, Mason Disick.

The Hulu star shared a few snaps of a walk she recently took around the California neighborhood with Mason, 13, and her other son, Reign, 8.

On her Instagram Stories, Kourtney, 44, first shared a pic of Reign putting on a funny face.

Scott Disick's son held his phone in one hand while the other was on his chin.

He sported a long-sleeved blue shirt, gray shorts, and a pair of Crocs with aliens on them.

She captioned the pic: "Night walk."

In the following Story, she took a blurry picture of Mason wearing a gray sweatshirt, black pants, and sneakers looking off to the side.

"With my boys," Kourtney said.

LOW-KEY TEEN

Mason has been shying away from the limelight recently and doesn't even appear on the family's Hulu series, The Kardashians, despite his siblings being on the show.

Kourtney and Scott, 40, are also parents to their 11-year-old daughter, Penelope.

Mason was recently snapped with his dad and siblings while celebrating Father's Day.

He kept his back to the camera, though he still looked barely recognizable from behind with his tall height and shaggy hair.

The group visited celeb-favorite Nobu for a meal to commemorate the special day.

Days earlier, Kourtney threw her son and his friends a lavish party at their $9million mansion to close out the school year.

The bash included a Raising Cane's food truck serving chicken fingers and other fast food.

Kourtney also ordered an abundance of pizza and took a snap of them lined up in the kitchen.

'HE DOES NOT LIKE IT'

Mason is one of the few Kardashian-Jenner kids not photographed with their famous parents.

During an appearance on the Not Skinny But Not Fat podcast in September, Kourtney explained why her son prefers life away from the cameras.

"I see comments all the time that I'm never with my kids," Kourtney said.

"And I just don't love to post my kids on social media. They don't love it, sometimes they do.

"Mason doesn't love it, I feel like," podcast host Amanda Hirsch remarked.

Kourtney replied: "He does not like it. He doesn't want any part of it. He's not on social media.

"He doesn't love it, and I want my kids to be kids. I crave normalcy as much as we can have it."

BABY NEWS

And Kourtney's brood is only growing after she announced earlier this month that she's pregnant with her fourth child.

She shared the news from the crowd of a Blink-182 concert, in which Travis, 47, is the drummer.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum held up a sign that read, "Travis I'm Pregnant" - a nod to the band's All The Small Things music video where an attractive groupie flashed a similar sign while rocking out to the music.
