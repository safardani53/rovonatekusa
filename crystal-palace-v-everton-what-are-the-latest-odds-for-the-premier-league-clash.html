Crystal Palace v Everton: What are the latest odds for the Premier League clash?

April 24, 2024
blog

AFTER a 4-0 thrashing of Manchester City, Everton head to Selhurst Park in confident mood.

Ronald Koeman will hope his side can replicate that performance against a struggling Crystal Palace side.


Keep up to date with ALL the latest Everton and Crystal Palace news, gossip, transfers and goals on our club page plus fixtures, results and live match commentary.

Who is going to win? Latest odds

Crystal Palace - 21/10

Everton - 11/8

Draw - 11/5

SunSport prediction

A 4-0 win over Manchester City will give Everton all the confidence they need and against a struggling Crystal Palace side, you'd expect them to seal all three points again. Everton to win 2-1

What is the latest Crystal Palace team news?

New £12 million signing Jeffrey Schlupp will be hoping for a first start after coming on as a late sub in the 3-0 defeat to West Ham. Sam Allardcye will look to avoid too many changes as they try and fight their way away from the relegation zone.

Who is definitely out for Crystal Palace?

Bakary Sako and Wilfired Zaha are currently away at the African Cup of Nations. Steve Mandanda, James McArthur, Pape Soure and Connor Wickham are all still missing through injury.

What is the latest Everton team news?

Manager Ronald Koeman will be keen to keep the same side that won comfortably over Manchester City with youngster Tom Davies impressing. New signing from Manchester United, Morgan Schneiderlin, will be pushing for a first start.

Who is definitely out for Everton?

Yannick Bolasie, Dominic Calvert-Lewin and Muhammed Besic continue to miss out through injury and Idrissa Gana Gueye is at the African Cup of Nations with Senegal.

What they say

Sam Allardyce realised how big of a task he's got at Crystal Palace after their 3-0 defeat to West Ham: "We don't have the capability to make sure we don't lose. That's the first thing to put right at a struggling club and at the moment we don't know how to get that result."

Ronald Koeman was obviously delighted with Everton's performance on Sunday: "I think it is a big compliment to Everton today - the organisation defensively. It makes the final result and the way we played perfect."

When is Crystal Palace vs Everton?

Sam Allardyce will be hoping to end the seven game run without a win when the host Everton on Saturday January 21 at 3pm.

Where can I watch the game?

The game is not on TV but you can keep up-to-date with all the action on the Sun Football App.

You can also follow the action on the SunSport live blog.

Alternatively, you can listen to live radio commentary on talkSPORT2 from 2pm.

Crystal Palace vs Everton - Facts

  • The Eagles are 15 points worse off after 21 games this season (16) than they were at this point last season (31).
  • Everton have had 17 different teenagers score for them in Premier League history, a record in the competition. Tom Davies and Ademola Lookman both netted in the Toffee's last Premier League game vs Manchester City.
  • Crystal Palace haven't scored a home goal against Everton in any of their last three meetings with them, since Mark Hudson scored in a 1-3 defeat in August 2004.
  • The Eagles have lost just one of their last seven Premier League meetings with Everton (W2 D4), with the last three all ending level.
  • However, Everton haven't lost to Palace at Selhurst Park in the league since October 1994, winning three and drawing two since.
