B&amp;M is selling a Mini Millions sweet dispenser for 2.99... and shoppers are going wild for it

August 31, 2024
blog

SWEET lovers are going wild for a Millions sweet dispenser at bargain store B&M that lets you have the tasty treats on tap.

The mini machine has multiple pumps so you can top up your machine with your favourite flavours.

The best part of the delicious device is that it costs a purse-pleasing £2.99, and shoppers are being quick to stock up on them.

The toy comes in blue and pink, and is stocked with the Millions flavours bubblegum, strawberry and sour strawberry.

The budget brand posted on its Facebook page saying: "Our Mini Millions Sweets machines are FLYING off the shelves right now and we can see why!

"They're a great treat for kids of ALL ages (and big kids too) allowing you to dispense your favourite tiny tasty chewy sweets!"

One shopper gushed: “Got one last week. They are brilliant.”

Another commented on the post saying: “Must get one of these!”

It’s not the first time B&M has released a dispenser themed on Millions.

They previously released a £12.99 version, which was so popular it was being flogged on eBay for almost double its original price.

“We can reassure our customers that more stock is on its way and will be hitting the shelves soon, so don’t panic – we don’t want any disappointed children this Christmas”.
