Who is Ato Boldon:

Ato Boldon is a famous Runner. He was born on December 30, 1973 and his birthplace is Port of Spain, Trinidad And Tobago. Ato is also well known as, Legendary former sprinter who won four Olympic medals and set national records in the 50, 60, 100 and 200 meter events. Following his illustrious athletic career, he became a track and field analyst for NBC and ESPN.

Ato is originated from Trinidad And Tobago. He and American sprinter Tyson Gay are both World Champions in the men’s 200 meter race.
