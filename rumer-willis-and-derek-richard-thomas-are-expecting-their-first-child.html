Rumer Willis And Derek Richard Thomas Are Expecting Their First Child

February 26, 2024
blog

Rumer Willis and Derek Richard Thomas are expecting their first child. The former announced the news on social media with a photo of Thomas cuddling her baby belly. Thomas made an identical post on his Instagram account. The captions included a seedling emoji. Both postings’ comments sections were inundated with good wishes from their close friends and followers. The couple did not say whether they were expecting a boy or a girl. Rumer claimed on The Talk two years ago that her father, Bruce Willis, wanted her to have a son since the family already had a lot of girls.

Rumer Willis, on the other hand, said that she did not hurry into extending her family and likes to wait for some time before making such choices.

Derek Richard Thomas is a musician and Vista Kicks member.

Derek Richard Thomas rose to prominence as a musician, going under the stage moniker DRT. He also plays with the band Vista Kicks. Vista Kicks is well-known for albums such as B*oty Shakers Ball and Twenty Something Nightmare. They’ve played a variety of events, including opening for the Rolling Stones in Santa Clara’s Levi’s Stadium in 2019.

They subsequently worked with vocalist Audra Mae on an album called Chateau Mae Mae, which was published in 2020. Derek has previously worked as a music producer for the band The Hail Maries. Thomas hasn’t divulged anything about his educational history, work, or parents. Despite his popularity, he does not have a Wikipedia page, hence many facts about his life are unavailable. His Instagram account is active, with 123 posts and 7,480 followers. His photos and videos generally show him performing on different occasions.

In May 2022, Rumer Willis alluded to her connection with Derek Richard Thomas.

Rumer Willis and Derek Richard Thomas have been romantically associated since 2020, albeit it is unclear how they initially met. Willis stated last month that she was dating Thomas. The actress shared a few photos on Instagram of the couple enjoying their time together in nature. Rumors about their connection emerged when Thomas paid homage to Willis on her birthday, saying she had brought him “joy and calm.”

Rumer hinted at her connection in May 2022, when she praised Derek as one of the most gifted singers and composers she had ever heard. Rumer Willis is best known for her role as Gia Mannetti in the iconic television series 90210. She also appeared in the Fox drama series Empire as Tory Ash. She has appeared in various additional films and television series, and she won Dancing with the Stars in 2015. Her father, Bruce Willis, and mother, Demi Moore, have both worked in the entertainment world for many years.
