I'm a Celeb winner odds revealed - with two stars battling it out to take this year's jungle crown

May 28, 2024
blog

I'M A Celebrity is shaping up to be a two horse race, according to bookies.

There's yet to be a public elimination after two stars - Grace Dent and Jamie Lynn Spears - quit the show on medical grounds.

But that hasn't stopped a couple of names breaking away from the pack as punter money flows in.

As it stands, Made In Chelsea favourite Sam Thompson is in pole position with bookmaker Coral with odds of 11-8.

He's followed by This Morning star Josie Gibson whose odds have been cut from 9-4 to 7-4.

Political figure Nigel Farage has had a surge of interest going from 14-1 to 10-1.

Coral’s John Hill said: “Jose Gibson has attracted strong support in our betting over the last couple of days. She is now just behind Sam Thompson in the outright market, in what is starting to look like a two-horse race between the two popular contestants."

Hollyoaks' Nick Pickard is a rank outsider at 150-1 followed by Nella Rose and Marvin Humes at 100-1.

Last night Sam opened up about his ADHD diagnosis and the support he receives from girlfriend Zara McDermott.

People with the condition can be hyperactive, restless and have trouble concentrating.

In a conversation with Josie and Marvin, the reality star said: "There's loads of people who are actually like of a certain age who get diagnosed way later.

"When the low bits happen it goes low, but then the highs are really high."

Heaping praise on his girlfriend, Sam continued: "My girlfriend Zara is just so steady and it is so good for me.

"When she goes steady I go like that [up and down] so it is so nice to have someone keeping you going.

"My time keeping is terrible and sometimes I get quite selfish and it's not just with her it's with everyone.. my listening to Zara whenever she's talking, sometimes Zara will listen to me and I'll come back full of energy like 'oh you'll never guess what I've just done' and I'll say what have you done to her and as she's explaining I find myself staring off at something completely different.

"She will be like 'you're not even listening to me' and sometimes you feel like a bit of a knob."

Josie interjected and said: "But you would not be Sam without it."

Afterwards in the bush telegraph, Marvin said: "He is literally one of the most fascinating people I've ever met."
