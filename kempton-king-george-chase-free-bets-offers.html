Kempton's King George VI Chase free bets and offers for racing this Boxing Day

September 25, 2024

YES, Christmas Day is great and all, but Boxing Day is a day for sport - headlined by the King George VI Chase at Kempton.

It's a race packed with immense quality with no real standout, clear-cut winner in the field, which makes it just as hotly-anticipated as normal.

King George Chase preview

It's a festive spectacular every year and now the King George is in its 73rd renewal.

It's the most prestigious chase of the winter and is the highlight of what is a packed day of racing - with £142,375 up for grabs for winning connections.

The three mile trip will test the stamina of those that take it on, with the chance to join an elite list of previous winners including Kauto Star, Desert Orchid and Best Mate.

This time round, it's Allaho who looks set to go off as favourite after Willie Mullins confirmed that was the race they were targeting.

The Cheveley Park-owned horse missed all of last season with injury though returned at Clonmel with a win last month, taking him to five wins on the bounce.

Bravemansgame is the current second favourite though hasn't won at all in 2023.

He was comfortably beaten by Royale Pagaille in the Betfair Chase last time out, though his trainer Paul Nicholls has a stellar record in this race.

Nicholls has scooped first place 11 times since 2006 - including four of the last five renewals with Clan Des Obeaux, Frodon and Bravemansgame.

Gerri Colombe was the long-time antepost leader but has drifted as they dither on a final decision whether or not to run.

Shishkin is in the line-up and his reluctance to race last time out leaves big question marks over him and his temperament.

The Real Whacker, Royale Pagaille and Hewick are all interesting contenders for different reasons at bigger prices.

    There’s plenty to consider when choosing the best horse racing betting app, as sometimes top betting sites don’t always translate to the smaller ‘on the go’ format of the smartphone or tablet betting app.

    We’ve picked out our top things to look our for when choosing a bookie with a betting app below:

    • Speed: The main plus of using a horse racing betting app is the ability to bet quickly on the go, which means you want an interface which is quick and easy to navigate. Make sure you look out for reviews of betting apps to get a feel for which providers offer the fastest and simplest experience.
    • Offering: Although you’re ideally looking for a speedy app, you don’t want to sacrifice your betting experience for it and you still need to look out for apps which offer as many sportsbook markets as you need. For example, if you’re a distances punter, it would not be helpful to choose an app which tends to only offer more simplified betting – like win only, each-way and exacta bets.
    • It's also a good idea to check out what else the operator has on their website. In addition to sports bonus offers, some sites also have casino bonus offers. Bear in mind that each casino offer will likely have a wagering requirement and be limited to one household, one IP address, and one phone number. 
    • Streaming: If you’re betting on the go, you’re going to want to be able to watch the racing on the go, too. You want a betting app that is going to be able to reliably show you coverage of all the racing in Britain and Ireland – with anything overseas a big bonus.
