See Teen Mom Jenelle Evans' humble North Carolina property including a modular home, she-shed, barn

May 17, 2024

TEEN Mom 2 alum Jenelle Evans lives in a humble North Carolina property.

The 29-year-old's property includes a modular home, she-shed, barn, inground pool and more amenities.

Whether she's vlogging on YouTube or sharing a dance video on TikTok, Jenelle has given fans glimpses into her North Carolina home over the years.

The home is settled on a large property of land, while a walkway allows the Teen Mom 2 alum's family to get from the street to the front door without walking on the grass.

Back in January, Jenelle shared a YouTube video that showed her and husband, David Eason, building their home's she-shed.

In the clip, the MTV star explained that she will use the she-shed to record podcasts.

The couple worked hard to put down the hardwood floors and paint the ceiling white.

Jenelle and David furnished the she-shed by adding two chairs, a coffee table and a lamp.

Additionally, Jenelle explained that the room will be complete with a microphone and tripod stands for the podcasting setup.

The outside of the home includes a nice porch with two chairs and a brown fence, which allows the family to spend quality time outside.

Jenelle has also given fans a look into the inside of her home, which includes mismatched hardwood floors and a living room furnished with a large dresser and two-seater couch.

The family also likely spends a good amount of time at the pool table inside.

Meanwhile, the outside of the home includes a barn with plenty of farm animals.

Jenelle regularly takes to social media to show off the animals on their property, while she's also been known to share snaps of the vegetables they have grown in their yard.

After working hard to provide food for the family at the barn, Jenelle and David can cool off in their inground pool.

The Teen Mom 2 alum has posted a number of TikTok videos near the pool, which capture her showing off her dance skills in swimsuits.

Jenelle and David tied the knot in 2017, while they share four-year-old daughter Ensley together.

The former reality star is also the mother to 11-year-old son Jace with ex Andrew Lewis and sic-year-old son Kaiser with ex Nathan Griffith.

In an exclusive interview with The Sun, Jenelle recently admitted her oldest son Jace's behavior has been "bad and rebellious" after previously claiming in court papers he "set fire" to his grandmother's home.

The mom of three has been in a court battle with her mother, Barbara Evans, over custody of the 11-year-old.
