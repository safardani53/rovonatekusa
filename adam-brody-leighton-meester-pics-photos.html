Leighton Meester and Adam Brody are such an adorable couple. Celebrate Adam’s birthday with their sweetest photos of all time.

Leighton Meester and Adam Brody may not be flashy about their relationship, but they are one of Hollywood’s most long-standing couples. The duo met while filming their movie The Oranges in 2010 and quickly started dating. The pair got engaged in November 2013 and married the following February 2014.

Growing into a family, the two became a trio when they welcomed their daughter, Arlo Day Brody, in August 2015. Their second child was born in 2020. The former teen stars even flirted with workplace romance when Adam guest starred on Leighton’s series Single Parents (2018-2020.)

Leighton and Adam have made it a habit of keeping their relationship out of the spotlight. But over the years, the two have had their fair share of red carpet outings, like here at the premiere of his show Startup in 2016.
