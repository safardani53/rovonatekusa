Who is Chris McCausland and what is his net worth?

September 11, 2024
blog

OVER the years Chris McCausland has become a familiar face on TV, especially on comedy shows.

But some fans may not know that he once appeared in a very famous British soap.

What is Chris McCausland known for?

Born in Liverpool on June 15, 1977 Chris McCausland is a comedian and actor.

The TV star is blind due to retinitis pigmentosa.

Chris first broke onto the comedy circuit in 2013 after performing stand up in Balham, London.

He regularly appears at comedy venues around the UK, including The Comedy Store

What is Chris McCausland's net worth?

Chris has become a familiar face on TV over the years, and has seen his net worth grow as a consequence.

According to website Net Worth Post, Chris is reportedly worth $1.1 million, which is over £800,000.

As well as TV appearances, Chris also makes money from his stand-up work.

Is Chris McCausland married and does he have children?

Chris is private when it comes to his personal life.

Most read in TV

However, we do know he is married to wife Patricia.

The couple also have a daughter called Sophie.

What TV shows has Chris McCausland been in?

Chris has gone on to appear on a number of comedy shows including Would I Lie to You? and Have I Got News For You.

He will also be known to fans of CBeebies show Me Too! thanks to his role as Rudi.

Soap fans might recognise him from a guest appearance on EastEnders in 2018 when he played Karl, a blind man who took on Bernadette Taylor at chess.

Chris appeared on 8 Out of 10 Cats Does Countdown, and scored highly.

This was even more impressive considering he was playing from memory alone, and at the end of the show, he got the Countdown Conundrum before anyone else.

In 2021 Chris also appeared on Celebrity Antiques Road Trip alongside Rufus Hound in a bid to make as much money for charity as possible.

In 2023 he took part in Channel 4's Scared of the Dark.
