Nicole Kidman has rocked a number of hairstyles over the years. From her natural red curls to her straight blonde hair, the actress is always keeping us on our toes. The Oscar winner has never been afraid to take risks when it comes to hair makeovers. She’s chopped off her long locks, rocked bangs, and more. When she first became famous, Nicole was showing off her natural red curls. Over the years, she tried out a number of different looks and went for more relaxed hairstyles. While straight hair is nothing new, Nicole’s straight hair with her gorgeous red gown at the 2007 Oscars became an iconic look. However, with her new role in ‘The Undoing,’ she’s throwing it way back. Nicole’s got tight red curls for her latest role, and it feels like it’s the 1990s again.
